I N S T A N T U S A G E R E W A R D

AT MEME MAKER DAO WE AIM TO CREATE AN ECOSYSTEM WHERE MEME MAKERS ARE INCENTIVIZED TO DISPLAY THEIR WORK ON OUR MARKETPLACE AND GET RATED FOR THE QUALITY OF THEIR WORK. THE WORK THEY DISPLAY WILL BE SEEN BY PROSPECTIVE CLIENTS WHO CAN THEN APPROACH THEM TO GET MEMES MADE FOR THEIR PROJECT. THINK OF IT AS AN UPWORK/FIVER ON BLOCKCHAIN.

OUR AIM IS TO START WITH MEME MAKERS AND THEN GROW IT AS A FULL FLEDGE MARKETPLACE FOR ANYTHING RELATED TO BLOCKCHAIN, WHICH INCLUDES SMART CONTRACT DEVELOPMENT, NFTS, HIRING ADMINS/MODS, HYPE MEN, WEBSITE DEVELOPERS AND INFLUENCERS. BY PROVIDING ALL THESE SERVICES UNDER OUR MARKETPLACE WE WILL BRIDGE THE GAP BETWEEN TRUST WORTHY RESOURCES AND CRYPTO PROJECTS LOOKING FOR RELIABLE SUPPORT.
